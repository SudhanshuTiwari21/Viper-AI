Viper lets you choose how much capability to spend on each conversation. The tier control is next to chat—pick what fits the task.

## Auto

**Good default** for everyday work: explanations, small edits, navigation, and back-and-forth. Use Auto when you want responsive answers without always reaching for the strongest model.

## Premium

**Heavier reasoning** for hard problems: tricky bugs, large refactors, architecture choices, or long-context understanding. Expect somewhat higher latency or cost depending on your organization’s setup.

## Choosing

- Start on **Auto**; move to **Premium** when quality matters more than speed.
- If answers feel shallow, try Premium for that thread only.
- Exact availability and limits depend on your **plan and backend configuration**—your admin or internal docs may add org-specific rules.
