Viper is an **AI-native workspace** for people who ship software: you open your project, chat with context from your repo, and move faster without juggling five different tools.

## What you can do here

- **Stay in flow** — Ask questions, outline a plan, debug an error, or let the agent take multi-step actions, all from one chat next to your editor.
- **Use your real codebase** — Viper works with the folder you open so answers reflect your files, structure, and constraints—not a generic tutorial.
- **Choose how the AI behaves** — Switch between lightweight questions and deeper reasoning when the task needs it (see [Chat](/docs/guide/chat) and [Models](/docs/guide/models)).

## Who it’s for

Product-minded engineers, tech leads, and builders who want **one place** to think, write, and execute—not a separate doc tool, ticket system, and IDE extension that barely talk to each other.
